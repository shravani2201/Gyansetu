Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on GyanSetu?

Creating an account is simple:

  1. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
  2. Fill in your basic information
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Complete your profile

What are the system requirements for using GyanSetu?

GyanSetu is optimized for low-bandwidth environments. Minimum requirements:

  • Internet connection: 2G or better
  • Browser: Latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
  • Device: Any smartphone, tablet, or computer

Are the courses available in multiple languages?

Yes, our courses are available in multiple regional languages including:

  • Hindi
  • Bengali
  • Telugu
  • Tamil
  • Marathi
  • And more...

How can I access content offline?

To access content offline:

  1. Look for the download icon next to the course material
  2. Click to download while you have internet connection
  3. Access the content later from your "Downloads" section
